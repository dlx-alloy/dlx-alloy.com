  • FeCrAl Series

FeCrAl alloy has the characteristic of high resistivity, low temperature resistance coefficient, high operating temperature, good anti-oxidation and anti-corrosion under high temperature.

It is widely used in industrial furnace, household appliances, metallurgy, machinery, aircraft, automotive, military and other industries producing heating elements and resistance elements.
